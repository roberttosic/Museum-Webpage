Le Louvre is the largest art museum in the world. It is a historic monument in Paris, and a central landmark for Paris. In 2017, Le Louvre was considered the world's most visited art museum.
<br><br>
The museum is inside the Louvre Palace. Louvre Palace was made as a fortress under Philip II. It then started to lose its defensive capabilities, and then was converted into the main residence for the French Kings. The building has been endlessly extended with the various kings that kept coming in and out. When the French Revolution, Le Louvre got turned into a museum to help commemorate the nation's masterpieces.
