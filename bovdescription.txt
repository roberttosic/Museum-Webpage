The Birth of Venus was made by Sandro Botticelli was made roughly in the mid 1480s. The painting shows Venus coming to the show after her birth, where she came out the sea as a full grown adult.
                    <br><br>
                    The painting is generally linked with Bottcelli's other mythological painting, the Primavera. Unlike the Primavera, the meaning and themes behind the painting are very obvious.
