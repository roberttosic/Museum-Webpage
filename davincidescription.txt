Leonardo Da Vinci was born on April 15, 1452 in present day Italy, and died on May 2, 1519.
<br><br>
Leonardo dabbled in areas like invention, painting, sculpting, architecture, science, math, engineering, among many others. He is considered one of the greatest painters to ever live, if not the greatest.
<br><br>
He is also credited with inventions like the parachute, helicopter, and tank. He is regarded almost as someone who is superhuman, one of the most diversely talented people to ever live.
