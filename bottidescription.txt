Alessandro di Mariano di Vanni Filipepi, also known as Sandro Botticelli, was born in Italy sometime in 1445, and died on May 17, 1510.
<br><br>
Botteicelli was known to be one of the big inspirations for what we consider to be Early Renaissance era painting. He was very big into creating religious pieces, and constantly deviated from the mainstream style of Italian painting at the time.
