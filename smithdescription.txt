The Smithsonian Museum, or musueums, consists of 19 museums. The large majority of these musumes are located in Washington D.C. The Smithsonian museums are visited by more than 25 million people every single year.

The birth of the Smithsonian Institution goes back to James Smithson's legacy going to the United States in 1826. The $500,000 dollars went to established the Smithsonian Institution. It originally started to house an art gallery, a library, and a chemical laboratory.
